Job description
Ben jij een bedreven en betrokken (cliënt)vertrouwenspersoon Wet Zorg en Dwang (WZD)? Heb jij een afgeronde opleiding in de richting van MWD, SJD, psychologie, rechten of verpleegkundige én wil je meer maatschappelijk betrokken zijn? Lees dan snel verder. 
Whatdo we need
Als (cliënt)vertrouwenspersoon WZD neem je het volgende mee: 
  • HBO diploma (preferably you have a diploma in MWD, SJD, psychology, law or nursing)
  • Driver's license and own transport
  • You have knowledge of client rights and laws and regulations in healthcare
  • Preferably you have demonstrable experience in a healthcare or legal environment
  • Flexibility; You can sometimes work (partly) in the evening
Watga je doen?
Are you an adept and involved (client) confidential advisor Wet Zorg en Dwang (WZD)? Do you have a completed education in the direction of MWD, SDJ, psychology, law or nursing and do you want to be more socially involved?
  • Support clients who (will) receive involuntary care
  • You put their interests and those of their representatives first
  • You offer a listening ear for questions or problems and try to offer customized support where possible
  • As a confidential advisor, you not only advise and inform your clients by telephone, but you also visit them in the healthcare institutions where they stay
  • You have an eye for the rights of the clients and, where necessary, discuss the bottlenecks with the healthcare provider
What
do you get

Good to mention right away: this function is based on Recruitment & Selection. So you will be employed directly by our client! Joinuz only supports, as mentioned earlier, with the Recruitment & Selection.

Good salary

But of course it also depends on your work experience and wishes. This position is scaled to scale 8 of the Collective Labor Agreement for Social Work

Guarantees

As a confidential advisor you will receive a contract for the duration of 1 year, with the possibility of extension

Growth

You will receive a wide range of intervision, courses and training

Remote work

The option to work from home (with allowances and the right supplies) or at one of the locations
