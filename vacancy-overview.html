Working through Joinuz:
here's what to expect

Working through Joinuz means the best of both worlds. As a secondment agency, we offer you the security of permanent employment combined with the flexibility to gain experience at different organizations. With personal guidance, a sharp match, and access to our network, we help you find opportunities that truly fit.

The right match

Work that genuinely fits. We look beyond your CV and connect you to assignments and organizations that align with your ambitions and needs.

Permanent & flexible

Security and variety. You become a Joinuz employee and combine a stable income with the flexibility to work at different organizations.

Personal guidance

Always someone thinking along with you. From the first meeting to your next step: we guide you personally through every stage of your career.

Access to new opportunities

More possibilities than you'd find on your own. Through our network, we open doors to assignments and employers that often aren't publicly visible.

Zo werkt het bij Joinuz

  1. 1

    Let's start

    Please send us your CV and contacts and our journey with you will start.

  2. 2

    First contact

    Our recruiter checks your CV, and after it we will contact you within 3 working days for feedback.

  3. 3

    Personal introduction

    Depending on that feedback, you will be invited for a personal introduction where we map out all your wishes.

  4. 4

    Drawing up the contract

    If you decide to take the job after that introduction, we will draw up a contract. After it has been signed by both you and us, you can get started.
